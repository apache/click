Who we are

Apache Click operates on merit: the more you do, the more responsibility you will obtain.

A successful project requires many people to play many roles. Some members write code or documentation, while others are valuable as testers, submitting patches and suggestions.

The team is comprised of a Project Management Committee (PMC), Committers and Contributors.

PMC members and Committers have direct access to the source of the project and actively evolve the code-base.

Contributors improve the project through submission of patches and suggestions to the PMC and Committers. The number of Contributors to the project is unbounded.

Get involved today. All contributions to the project are greatly appreciated.
